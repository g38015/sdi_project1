My story is about making a decision on where to go skiing.

I am selecting the ski resort based on if it snowed the night before. If it snowed 10 inches or more then I am going to Kirkwood. If it snowed less than 10 inches I am going to Sierra

If it did not snow the decision is made to go to Sierra at Tahoe.
